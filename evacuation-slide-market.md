<p><ul><li>Single-Lane Type</li><li>Dual-Lane Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The evacuation slide market is categorized into two main types: single-lane and dual-lane slides. Single-lane slides are designed for rapid egress of passengers, allowing one person to exit at a time, making them suitable for smaller aircraft. In contrast, dual-lane slides accommodate two passengers simultaneously, facilitating quicker evacuations in larger aircraft or high-traffic situations. Both types are essential for ensuring passenger safety during emergencies, with design considerations focusing on efficiency, speed, and compliance with aviation regulations.</p></p>
