<p><p>The blood pressure disorders drug market comprises several types of medications. Diuretics help reduce blood pressure by expelling excess salt and water from the body. Angiotensin Converting Enzyme (ACE) inhibitors block the formation of a blood vessel constrictor, promoting relaxation and lowering blood pressure. Angiotensin Receptor Blockers (ARBs) prevent angiotensin from binding to its receptors, also aiding in blood pressure reduction. Additionally, "Others" include various alternative antihypertensive drugs, such as beta-blockers and calcium channel blockers, each with distinct mechanisms.</p></p>
