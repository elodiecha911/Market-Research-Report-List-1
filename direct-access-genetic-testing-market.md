<p><p>The Direct-Access Genetic Testing market encompasses various segments, including diagnostic screening, preimplantation genetic diagnosis (PGD), and relationship testing. Diagnostic screening provides individuals with information about genetic predispositions to diseases, enabling early intervention. PGD allows couples undergoing in vitro fertilization to test embryos for genetic disorders before implantation. Relationship testing, commonly involving paternity tests, determines biological relationships between individuals. These services enhance personal health decisions, reproductive planning, and familial understanding, driving demand in the growing genetic testing landscape.</p></p>
