<p><strong>The Venom Antiserum Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyvalent</li><li>Monovalent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Venom Antiserum Market is categorized into two main types: polyvalent and monovalent antiserums. Polyvalent antiserum is designed to neutralize multiple types of venom, providing broader protection against various snake species, making it valuable in regions with diverse venomous snakes. In contrast, monovalent antiserum targets a specific type of venom, offering high efficacy for particular species. The choice between these types depends on the local venomous fauna, the prevalence of snakebites, and the specific treatment requirements in different regions.</p></p>
